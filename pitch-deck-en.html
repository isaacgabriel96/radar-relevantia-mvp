From mass media to a fragmented era.

  • Limited target audience.

2000–2010

The first digital era

📱
2011–2020

The social media boom

  • Growing competition for attention.
  • Dependence on paid algorithms.
🤖
2021 and beyond

The fragmented era

  • Multiple platforms and audience segments.
  • ROI hard to measure.
  • New, fast-evolving channels.
